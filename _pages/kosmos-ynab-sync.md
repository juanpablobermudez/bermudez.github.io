*kosmos-ynab-sync* is a personal application used by the site
owner to import their own bank transaction data into
[YNAB](https://www.ynab.com) for personal budgeting. It is not
offered as a service to any third party.

## Terms of Use

The application is for the personal use of the developer only.
No warranty, express or implied, is provided. Use is at the
developer's own risk. The developer reserves the right to
modify or discontinue the application at any time.

## Privacy Policy

- The application accesses bank account information solely on
  behalf of the account holder (the developer), under the
  consent flow defined by the PSD2 Account Information Service
  (AIS) scope.
- Data retrieved is limited to booked transactions and account
  balances on accounts owned by the developer.
- All data is stored locally on the developer's own machine in
  CSV form. No data is transmitted to or stored on any
  third-party server operated by the developer.
- No data is shared with any third party.
- No analytics, tracking, or telemetry is collected by the
  application.
